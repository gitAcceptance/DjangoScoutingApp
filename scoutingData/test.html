Click on the Administrate tab at the top and then Users on the left.
The default is set on Last Name. Search criteria can be changed by clicking the Search by radial buttons
Sort by will reorder your results by the selected filter. Keep in mind that it will always sort by organization first, then by whatever you selected so your sorting may not have the desired results.
In organizations that have more than 100 users, users will be hidden by default.
The Page results filter is selected by default. This filter automatically limits the LMS to displaying 20 users per page. By deselecting this option, the LMS will return all results on one long page.
By default, Inactive accounts are hidden. If you want inactive accounts to display in your search, click the Show inactive users filter in the screen shot below.
Your most effective search is going to look something like this:Page Results is toggled off Show inactive users is toggled on At this point you can use ctrl+F to search the page quickly
Overview of User Profile Name and Username are read-only (can only be changed by Admins and Super Admins). Anyone can update the additional demographic information that has admin privileges or by the user themselves. (Log into their account and click on the Profile tab)
Make sure the account is set to Active before you give them login information.
